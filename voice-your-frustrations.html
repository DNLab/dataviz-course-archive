Assignment: Voice your Frustrations

We're getting to that point where the rubber meets the road, and "technical difficulties" are coming to the forefront of your experience.

 

The things I'm asking you to do in this course are technically challenging, and I know there will be roadblocks and frustrations along the way.

Please post in Slack, and copy the text as the submission for this assignment in Canvas.
