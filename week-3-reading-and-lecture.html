Please Read: Chapter 5 “Marks and Channels”

  • This, to me, is the most interesting and meaningful topic in data visualization. Please do a detailed reading of this particular chapter.

Please Read/Skim: Automating the Design of Graphical Presentations of Relational Information by Jock Mackinlay (PDF)

  • This seminal paper from 1986 shows a slightly different angle on the Marks and Channels concept, particularly the rankings. Please pay special attention to figures 14 and 15. This work eventually became Tableau.

If you did get a copy of "Semiology of Graphics"

  • Have a look through Chapter II. "The Properties of the graphic system". I recommend speed-reading this in the first pass, spending a few seconds on each page and taking in all the graphics.
